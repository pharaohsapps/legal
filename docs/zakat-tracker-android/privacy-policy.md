# Privacy Policy — Zakat Tracker:

*Last updated: April 2026*

**1. Overview**
Zakat Tracker ("the App") is developed by Pharaohs Apps ("we", "us"). This policy explains how the App handles your data.

**2. Data Storage**
All financial data you enter (assets, deposits, exchange rates, Zakat records) is stored exclusively on your device using an encrypted local database. No personal financial data is ever transmitted to our servers or any third party. We do not operate any backend server.

**3. Advertising (Free version only)**
The free version of Zakat Tracker includes advertisements served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers (such as the Advertising ID), app interaction data, and diagnostic information in order to serve ads and measure their performance. This data is governed by Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

To opt out of personalised advertising, you can reset or disable your device's Advertising ID in your Android settings (Settings → Privacy → Ads).

The Pro version of Zakat Tracker contains no advertisements and AdMob is not included.

**4. Live Exchange Rates**
The App fetches live exchange rate data from external financial sources (AlexBank FX, Yahoo Finance, gold/silver price feeds) over HTTPS. These requests contain no personal information — only the type of rate being requested.

**5. Notifications**
The App uses Android's notification system to alert you when Zakat may be due. No notification data is transmitted externally.

**6. Biometric Data**
Biometric authentication (fingerprint / face) is handled entirely by Android's BiometricPrompt API. The App never has access to your raw biometric data.

**7. Backup Files**
If you export a backup, the resulting `.zakat` file is encrypted with AES-256-GCM using a password you choose. We have no access to this file or its contents.

**8. Permissions**
The App requests only three permissions:
- `INTERNET` — to fetch live exchange rates
- `USE_BIOMETRIC` — to support fingerprint/face login
- `POST_NOTIFICATIONS` — to send Zakat due-date reminders

**9. Data Deletion**
All app data is stored locally. Uninstalling the App permanently removes all data from your device.

**10. Children's Privacy**
This App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

**11. Contact**
For privacy questions, contact us at: apps.pharaohs.consultancy@gmail.com
